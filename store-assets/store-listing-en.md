# Play Store Listing (English)

## App Name (max 30 chars)
FoodNeverArrives — Fake Delivery

## Short Description (max 80 chars)
A fake delivery app to open instead of the real one. Browse, "order," save calories.

## Full Description (max 4000 chars)
🍱 FoodNeverArrives lets you browse restaurants, build a cart, fill out a delivery form, and even watch a courier on a map — exactly like a real food delivery app.

The catch: **nothing is ever actually ordered, paid for, or delivered.**

📱 What is this for?
Next time you reach for a delivery app on autopilot, open this one instead. Browse the menus, put together a cart, hit checkout — the experience itself can be enough to satisfy the craving. At the end, the app shows you exactly how many **calories you just saved** by not ordering.

✨ Highlights
🍽️ 15 fictional restaurants, 40+ dishes (fried chicken, ramen, pasta, mala, sushi, pizza and more)
🛒 Full cart and options flow with spice level, size, and toppings — just like the real apps
🗺️ Live courier tracking on a fake map, in Rabbit (fast) or Turtle (slow) mode
🔥 "Calories saved" total shown after every fake order
🎨 4 color themes (orange, purple, blue, mint)
🌐 Instant Korean / English language toggle

⚠️ Please note
• This is a fully simulated demo app.
• No real orders, payments, or deliveries ever happen.
• All restaurants and menus are fictional.
• Anything you type in the delivery form stays on your device — nothing is sent to a server.

💛 From the developer
Food delivery apps are wonderfully convenient — which is exactly why we open them on autopilot. The fees, the food cost, the calories all add up faster than we think. FoodNeverArrives is a tiny pause button for that impulse. Have fun with it!
